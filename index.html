2023 NUS Chem•Comm competition

In 2023, I participated in the 2023 NUS Chem•Comm competition. Our team decided to focus and research on the effect of carbon capture unit (CCU) to make schools carbon neutral by 2030. This competition allowed me to develop and foster my interest in Chemistry, and especially on how we can help to protect our environment for the future generations. You can visit the website we designed for the competition here.

ISS

Our goal was to investigate the effect of colour on heat absorption of infra-red radiation. This project was an exciting first-time opportunity for me to go through an investigation and research process that spans from the planning, conducting the experiment, collection of the data and finally the analysis of the results.

ARTC camp

For the ARTC camp, we were tasked to come up with a product that could help increase the effectiveness of the recycling process. We did this by coming up with a device that could potentially detect which bottles were cleaned and properly washed before they go in the recycling bin. In this way, it would avoid contamination of recyclables that were already inside the bin.

2023 SYF competition

I was proud to be part of the SST Guitar Ensemble which won distinction at the 2023 SYF Competition. This was a golden opportunity for me to challenge myself to learn and play new and difficult pieces. It required commitment and dedication to perfect and master each song to the best of my abilities. At the same time, as the Vice Chairperson, I had to motivate and encourage my fellow guitarists to persevere in the gruelling practice sessions.

2024 Performance at Istana

The SST Guitar Ensemble was invited to perform at the Istana for the Hari Raya Haji. It was a very eye-opening experience for me as it was a chance for us to perform in front of a live audience. It was not an easy feat. We had to learn quite a number of pieces as our slot was for one hour, and we also had to perform outdoors in the sweltering heat.

2024 SUTD-SST-NP Big D Camp

The SUTD-SST-NP Big D Camp taught me how to work with my team members to come up with a prototype in a short period of time. Over 3 days, we had to keep focused on the task at hand as we had limited time to agree on an idea, come up with the product design and build the prototype.

IDC challenge

This challenge gave me the opportunity to work with my team-mates to build a disaster rescue robot. We worked on integrating an AI run on Raspberry Pi with the movement and control of the robot and sensors by making use of an Arduino Uno.

YCEP

Over the past three years, I have taken part in multiple YCEP events organised by CSA and different polytechnics. This allowed me to pick up useful cybersecurity skills that help me to learn to be safe when being online.

Computing performance task

My assigned Computing Performance Task was to use the Python programming and facial recognition libraries to detect the faces of users. The context was for it be a virtual photobooth for SST’s anniversary, hence eliminating the need for physical props. Link to the github repo.

Capstone

My year-end Capstone project sees me working with Heartware Network together with 3 of my friends to help our pioneer generation. Through this experience, I have nurtured my patience, compassion and understanding. I have gained a better insight about the elderly in our society, and in particular some of the unseen challenges that they face in their everyday life. So, our goal is to come up with an easy and simple solution that can contribute towards educating them to embrace technology.
