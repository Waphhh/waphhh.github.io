2023 NUS Chem•Comm competition

In 2023, I participated in the 2023 NUS Chem•Comm competition. Our team decided to focus and research on the effect of carbon capture unit (CCU) to make schools carbon neutral by 2030. This competition allowed me to foster and develop my interest for chemistry and especially in its ability to help keep our planet. You can visit our website here.

2023 SYF competition

Being part of the SST guitar ensemble, we obtained distinction in the 2023 SYF competiton. This opportunity allowed me to challenge myself to playing new and difficult pieces, it required the commitment and dedication to perfect and master each song to the best of my abilities.

2024 Performance at Istana

The SST guitar ensemble was invited to perform at the Istana for Hari Raya Haji! This performance was challenging but not in the same way as the SYF competition. This performance required us to learn a lot more songs but with the difficulty being lower.

2024 SUTD-SST-NP Big D Camp

During the SUTD-SST-NP Big D Camp, I was able to work with others to come up with a product to help people stay focused. Our prototype would remind its users using a sound to keep on task when it noticed a task has not been accomplished within the set time limit.

ARTC camp

For the ARTC camp, we were tasked with coming up with a prototype to help the recycling process. We did this by making a device that detected with bottles were cleaned and washed properly, to avoid contamination of recyclables already inside the bin.

IDC challenge

During this challenge, I got the opportunity to work with my teamates to build a disaster rescue robot. It required the integration of an AI ran on a Rapserry Pi together with the movement and control of the robot and sensors using an Arduino Uno.

ISS

We conducted an investigation on the effect of colour on heat absorbtion of infra-red radtiation. This was the first time I had gotten to go through an investigation and research process, from the research to the planning, conducting and finally the analysis of the results.
